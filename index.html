INDUSTRY-LEADING TRUCK EQUIPMENT SALES

At Custom Truck One Source, we are committed to providing a truly comprehensive outlet for all of your truck-related needs. We offer a genuine cradle-to-grave solution including the sale, rental, customization, remanufacturing, service and disposal of commercial trucks and heavy equipment. Browse our extensive range of new, remanufactured and used commercial trucks for sale and rental by equipment type or industry and don’t hesitate to call for details of our customization and maintenance services. With flexible in-house financing and lease agreements, we can meet the needs of your company, no matter its size or budget. See below or check our general FAQs for further information.

What makes you a single-source commercial truck and equipment provider?

Many dealers claim to be one-stop shops but we really are. Our range of services is designed to support companies in industries as diverse as construction, forestry, telecommunications and propane, over the full lifecycle of their commercial truck and trailer equipment. From the acquisition of a new or used truck, all the way through to the day you dispose of it, Custom Truck Once Source can meet your needs in full. We can sell or rent you a truck, customize it to your specifications, maintain it for you and when it reaches the end of its useful lifespan, we can either remanufacture it, buy it back from you, accept it as a trade-in for a new truck or sell it through our auction services. We go above and beyond everything that other commercial truck dealers offer.

Why should I come to Custom Truck One Source to rent or buy commercial trucks?

Apart from the fact you know we will be there to support you through the full lifecycle of your new truck, there are a number of reasons we are widely considered as the best when it comes to truck equipment sales in North America.

  • Competitive pricing – We work hard to ensure that prices for our new and used trucks, as well as our rental terms, remain highly competitive in all areas. Search for ‘commercial truck dealers or sales near me’, or just ‘truck equipment near me’ and take a look at what you find; you’ll quickly see that we offer exceptional value for money. Whether you are looking for trucks designed for the building supply, oil and gas, rail or utilities industry (or any other industry), we can offer you a great deal. To find out just how competitive we are, complete and submit our quote request form right now.
  • Specialized customization services – In addition to our range of standard commercial work trucks for sale, we also offer professional customization services. Buy or rent your trucks from us and you can be 100% sure they will be capable of doing exactly what you need them to.
  • Nationwide network – Our wide network of locations enables us to provide a first-class service to companies in all parts of the USA. Wherever you are and whatever your needs, you can rely on Custom Truck One Source to be there for you when it matters most. As you can see from the testimonials we receive, we don’t just promise a first-class, full lifecycle service, we deliver it too. Whether you’re looking for new commercial trucks for sale or a good rental deal, call and speak to us now.

How quickly can I get help if I have a problem with my truck equipment?

We provide a highly responsive maintenance and repair service to our customers in all areas of the United States, delivered by a team of fully qualified and experienced technicians. In the first instance, our customers have the option of calling our customer service center, which operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. This center is staffed by technicians with an in-depth knowledge of commercial work trucks so the person who answers each call can do a lot more than just take a message: they can provide professional advice and assistance too. If a problem cannot be resolved over the phone, we dispatch a technician to the site without delay. Thanks to our network of service bays across the USA and our dedicated team, we are normally able to reach even the remotest sites within 3 hours.
